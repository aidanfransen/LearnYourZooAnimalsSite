Rose-ringed Parakeet:

Rose-ringed Parakeet: Facts:

  1. At breeding time, they lay 3-4 eggs in tree cavities, rock crevices and buildings. It takes about 24 days for the eggs to hatch and the chicks grow very quickly.
  2. Rose-ringed parakeets have the largest range of any Old World parrot and can be found throughout northern Africa and southern Asia.
  3. They live in large groups and can be found in flocks of thousands of birds sometimes. They are very noisy and often mix with other birds.
  4. lso known as Indian ring-necked parakeets, these beautiful small parrots grow up to 40 cm long, which includes their tail feathers.
  5. In the wild, Rose-ringed Parakeets usually feed on buds, fruits, vegetables, nuts, berries and seeds.
