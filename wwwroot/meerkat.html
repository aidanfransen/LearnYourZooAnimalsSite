Meerkats:

Meerkat Facts:

  1. Meerkat when standing are about 30 cm tall
  2. Meerkats live in family group of about 20-50 meerkats
  3. One of the most important roles in the group for a meerkat is to stand as a sentry, or a watch guard. One meerkat will stand on its hind legs as a lookout while the rest of the group searches for food.
  4. Meerkats have adapted to live in the desert climate - dark patches around their eyes reduces glare helping them to see more easily.
  5. Meerkats are omnivores and eat insects mostly.
