Siberian Tiger Facts

  1. The Siberian Tiger is the largest cat in the world, measure 3.3m long on average, with a tail measuring 1 metre
  2. Adult males Siberian tigers weigh up to 320kg!
  3. Siberian Tigers are easily recognisable by their striped. Each tigers stripes are unique.
  4. Siberian tigers are solitary animals and mark their scent on trees
  5. Siberian Tugers are mostly found in Russia, as well as in China in small numbers
